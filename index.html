About Me

Hi! Welcome to my Portfolio. My name is Caroline and I currently reside in Adelaide, South Australia. I am studying at the University of Adelaide, participating in a Coding Bootcamp to hopefully gain some experience and knowledge to enter the world of Software Development.

I have had an interest in Web Development since I was as young as 11 years old. I started out playing around on Piczo with my own personal website, using HTML, CSS and a tiny bit of JavaScript. Since then I have had jobs where I was able to play with formulas, macros, and G-code; this is where I realised that I thoroughly enjoy working with codes and formulas to achieve a vast majority of outcomes.
